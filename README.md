## :pencil: Description

Mockup Magic is a Single Page Application  designed to take the headache out of brainstorming a color palette for your next app. It enables users to visit the site, and upon logging in they gain access to the random palette generator, powered by an API. The user is able to interact with the generator in multiple ways, they can rearrange the order of the palette, lock colors into place they like, and refresh new ones based off this input to their liking. Right below the palette made, is a real time updated wireframe or component the palette is applied to give the user an idea of what this scheme may look like on their own app.

## :atom_symbol: Getting Started

 ## How To Generate A Palette
    1. After Signing up with a unique email, you have access to your "Mockups" 
    and can navigate there, where the palette lives. 
    2. The Palette is simple and user friendly, simply press the spining arrows to generate
    a new palette.  
    3. Only like some of the colors? No problem! Lock those ones into place and generate
    a new palette utilizing those exact colors. 
    4. In the top right corner, you can name and save your favorite palletes. 
    5. Need to come back at a later date, no worries, you can access your saved palettes
    to the left in a menu.
